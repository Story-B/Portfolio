By observing these word clouds, we can see that James Monroe used the words “Spain” “savages” “tribes” and “power” multiple times each. From this we can deduce that there was likely conflict with another group. Buchanan's cloud shows the words “people” “great” “free” and “domestic” multiple times each, this could lead us to believe the country was more focused on internal affairs.
